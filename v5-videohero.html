The Story

Some promises are stronger than distance.

Sonia always imagined her grandfather walking her down the aisle. But the years and an ocean stood in the way. So a team of engineers at Network School asked: what if presence didn’t require a plane?

In 48 hours, they built a humanoid robot her grandfather could control remotely — his face on the screen, his voice in her ear, his blessing on every step.

The Build

48 Hours. One Chance.
