So they sent a robot in his place.

The Story

The bride always dreamed of her grandfather walking her down the aisle. He was thousands of miles away, unable to make the journey. The distance was an ocean. The absence, a heartbreak.

At Network School in Forest City, Malaysia, a community of builders heard the wish and got to work. They outfitted a humanoid robot with live video and audio, letting the grandfather see, speak, and walk alongside his granddaughter in real time from across the world.

At 6 PM on 26 June 2026, the robot walked the bride down the aisle at the wedding of Kris and Sonia. It was the world's first humanoid bridal escort. The grandfather was there after all.
