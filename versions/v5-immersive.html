I

She always dreamed of one thing.

Not the dress. Not the flowers.

She dreamed of her grandfather's hand

guiding her down the aisle.

 

But oceans stood between them.

Borders. Distance. Time.

The kind of separation that feels permanent.

 

So a community asked a simple question:

What if distance didn't matter?

 

They built a humanoid robot

that a grandfather could pilot from across the world —

seeing through its eyes, speaking through its voice,

walking beside her, step by step.

 

On June 26, 2026, in Forest City, Malaysia,

love found a way.
