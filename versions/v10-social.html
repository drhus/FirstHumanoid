The World's First Humanoid Bridal Walk

26 · 6 · 26   6 PM

Some promises are
stronger than distance.

When her parents couldn’t cross the seas, we refused to let distance win.

He walked beside her through a humanoid — his face on the screen, his voice in her ear, his blessing on every step down the aisle.

The Wish

A daughter always pictured them at her side.

Sonia always imagined her parents walking her toward the rest of her life. But the years, and an ocean, stood in the way. So we asked: what if presence didn’t require a plane?

The Idea

Not a screen. Not a recording.
A presence.

The Build

48 Hours. One Chance.
