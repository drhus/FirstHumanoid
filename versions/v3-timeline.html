Chapter I

The Dream

Every bride dreams of this moment.

The music begins. The doors open. And there, beside her, is the person who held her hand when she first learned to walk — now walking her toward the rest of her life.

For Sonia, that person was her grandfather. The man who raised her spirit, who told her stories before sleep, who promised he would be there when the day came.

Chapter II

The Distance

But oceans separated them.

He couldn't make the crossing. Age, health, the vastness of the world between them — some distances feel impossible to bridge. The dream of walking her down the aisle seemed destined to remain just that: a dream.

And yet, in a small corner of Malaysia, a community was about to prove that some distances are only impossible until someone decides they're not.

Chapter III

The Idea

What if technology could bridge this distance?

At Network School in Forest City, Malaysia, there lives a community that doesn't just talk about the future — they build it. When they heard Sonia's story, they asked the only question that matters:

"What if her grandfather could still walk her down the aisle — through a humanoid robot he controls from across the world?"

A live video-audio connection. A robot that moves as he moves. His presence, real and unmistakable, walking beside her in the most important moment of her life.
